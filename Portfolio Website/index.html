Who I am

Full Stack Developer based out of California

I am a up and coming Full Stack Developer, based out Southern California with large interest in game development. I finished at the top of my class with a 4.0 at Bethel School of Technology and am currently taking a course in Unity3D. I co-developed a social networking application for Teen Challenge Alumni to help students stay in contact after graduating. I am currently investing time into creating my first mini game through Unity3D. Before I decided to change gears and aspire to being a computer programer, I use to own and operate my own business "Independent Mobility Inc." for about 9 years. I did everything from managing employees' workloads and projects, website design, dealt with all customer relations, managed employees' schedule, did all hiring, trained all employees on Quickbooks and shop projects, and managed all advertisement. Running a business was one of the most amazing experiences of my life. This company was a full service retail mobility scooter store, were we sold and serviced our own design of high-end wheel chairs. Our scooters were the fastest and would go the furthest on the market reaching up to 20mph and and getting up to 60 mile on one charge. Our modo was "Get your Freedom back!" and thats what we did. 90% of our customers were disabled or elderly and were completely dependent on others, and our scooter gave them a chance to be independent and giving them the freedom to whatever whenever. It was so great to see how many people we help in that community before having to shut the door due to COVID. It was very sad having to close the doors but the experience of running my own business I feel has prepared me for my next venture in life.
